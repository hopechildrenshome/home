Prathyasa Bal Bhawan

Home About Us Our Mission Facilities Gallery Activities News Contact

Located in the serene hills of Poundha village, Dehradun, Prathyasha Bal Bhawan is a vibrant children’s home run by the Hope for Children Society, dedicated to nurturing orphaned and vulnerable children with love, education, and dignity.

More than shelter, we offer a life—filled with learning, laughter, care, and growth. From academic support and spiritual grounding to animal care, life skills, and leadership, our children thrive in a setting that truly feels like family.

Join us in building lives that matter.
