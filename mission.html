Our Mission

"To Care for Needy and Underprivileged Children Irrespective of Caste or Creed and Provide for their Total Moral Development"

At Prathyasha Bal Bhawan, our mission is to provide a safe, loving, and spiritually nurturing home for orphaned, semi-orphaned, and vulnerable children—empowering them to rise above adversity and grow into compassionate, capable, and confident individuals.

We are committed to restoring childhood, dignity, and hope through holistic care—combining academic support, life skills, emotional healing, and spiritual grounding. Our goal is not just to shelter children, but to shape resilient, value-driven leaders for tomorrow.

Our Vision

To see every child, regardless of their background, experience love, safety, education, and opportunity—growing into a person of character, purpose, and hope, who will impact their community and the world for good.

Core Values
  • Compassion: We act with empathy and kindness toward every child.
  • Dignity: Every child deserves respect and the right to dream.
  • Faith: We are rooted in God’s love, trusting Him to guide and provide.
  • Integrity: We serve with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Excellence: We nurture academic, emotional, and spiritual growth.
  • Transformation: We believe in the potential of every child to rise, thrive, and lead.
