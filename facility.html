Our Facilities

Where comfort, care, and learning come together

Natural Environment & Playgrounds

Prathyasa Bal Bhawan is more than just a shelter—it is a vibrant, child-friendly space nestled in the peaceful hillside of Poundha village, Dehradun, surrounded by nature’s beauty and filled with opportunities for learning, play, and personal growth.

Our campus offers a spacious playground where children can run, play, and engage in a variety of sports and games. The grounds are dotted with mango trees and greenery, offering shade and a sense of freedom. We also have play equipment tailored for our younger children, making outdoor time both fun and safe.

Mini Animal Farm

What makes our home truly special is its miniature animal farm. Children learn responsibility and compassion by caring for our cows, ducks, pigeons, rabbits, guinea pigs, chickens, and even dogs.

Our aquarium of ornamental fishes adds beauty and calm to the space, while our well-kept gardens with flowering and decorative plants create a peaceful environment for reflection and learning.

Refreshing Pool Experience

Our campus features a well-maintained pool where children can enjoy supervised recreational time, build confidence in water, and develop essential swimming skills—all while having fun in a safe environment.

Accommodation & Kitchen

Our accommodation facilities are clean, safe, and well-ventilated, designed to give every child a sense of belonging. The kitchen is well-equipped, and our cook lovingly prepares nutritious and varied meals.

Children are also trained in cooking, cleaning, and basic maintenance—instilling life skills and self-reliance from a young age.

Scenic Location

Set against the backdrop of mountains, rivers, and scenic walking trails, Prathyasa Bal Bhawan offers an environment that naturally encourages health, exploration, and emotional well-being.

Holistic Growth

Our children excel not just in academics, but also in sports, arts, music, and other extracurricular activities—a reflection of the holistic care and opportunities they receive here.

At Prathyasa, every corner of the home is built with love, purpose, and a vision for a brighter future.
