About Us

Founded in 2005, Hope Haven is a non-profit organization that shelters and nurtures over 120 children. We aim to create a family-like environment and empower every child through education, counseling, and love.

Our Programs

  • 24/7 Shelter & Nutrition
  • Education & School Enrollment
  • Emotional & Mental Health Support
  • Vocational Training
  • Reintegration & Adoption Assistance

Our Mission

To provide care, education, and emotional support to children in need, helping them grow into confident, responsible individuals.Over the past year, we have made significant strides in advancing our mission of empowering poor and weak children and families.
