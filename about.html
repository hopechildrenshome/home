About Us

"A Home of Hope, Healing, and Growth"

Prathyasha Bal Bhawan, located in the serene hills of Poundha village, Dehradun, is a nurturing children’s home operated by the Hope for Children Society - a faith-led initiative committed to transforming the lives of underprivileged and vulnerable children.

Founded in 1991 on the values of compassion, dignity, and opportunity, our mission is to offer a safe, supportive, and loving environment where every child can grow intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually.

The vision for this home was born in the heart of Mr. John Varghese while he was a student at the Presbyterian Theological Seminary in Dehradun...

...A visit to Madras Colony — a community of rag pickers — deeply impacted him. After two years of outreach work, he realized that to truly transform these children's lives, they needed to be removed from environments of poverty and despair and raised in a morally and socially healthy space. Together with Mrs. Deepa Varghese, the dream became a reality.

At Prathyasha Bal Bhawan, we welcome children from broken families, orphaned or semi-orphaned backgrounds, and marginalized communities. Through structured care, mentoring, and holistic education, we help them reclaim hope and build a meaningful future...

Beyond academics, we focus on life skills, health, hygiene, character-building, and spiritual grounding. With the tireless dedication of our caretakers and the grace of God, Prathyasha Bal Bhawan continues to be a beacon of hope — one child, one life, one future at a time.

Who Are Rag Pickers?

You can find them in almost every Indian city — often in tattered clothes, with unwashed bodies, scavenging through garbage dumps...

...These children grow up abused, in extremely unhygienic and unsafe conditions, and are tragically vulnerable to every form of exploitation and evil.
